Why is it happened and what to do?

We tried to collect some of the possible reasons:
  • Rare Vehicle / Lack of Data

This vehicle is so rare that we haven’t found any acceptable data, such as dimensions, blueprints, photos or tech documentations.

For example, it could be 1899 Peugeot Coach which was produced in quantity of 1 unit and lost 80 years ago. Or some custom racing team’s formula car, or concept car shown once, or cinema vehicle or simply vehicle with a lack of information.

Solution:
Find some blueprints or at least acceptable photos on the web, scan your owner’s manual and brochures or make projection photos (like front, rear, side view) of your own vehicle by yourself.

Contact us and attach your pictures to an email.
  • Vehicle from the Future

This vehicle is not yet in production.

For example, you requesting to make 2025 Tesla Model Whatever which is only planned to be made in few years.

Solution:
In this case you could only wait till the real vehicle will be done and request one more time. Thank you.

Some other possible reasons are that this request is too general. So please try to make it specific. For example we can’t make “Ford blueprint request” because it is too general, but we can produce “Ford Focus blueprint request”.

In some cases we currently don’t produce this kind of blueprints (e.g. StarTrek Spaceship) or this vehicle does not exist.
